ABOUT THE GAME

Hot Road Infinite is an idle clicker game where you grow a collection of cars and watch them race along dynamic tracks. Upgrade vehicles, optimize your setup, and maximize earnings as your cars speed through loops and turns. Strategically manage your track to boost performance and unlock even more powerful rides.
