ABOUT THE GAME

DESCRIPTION

Pop colorful balloons and become the master of popping balloons

Pop the colorful balloons and have tons of fun. Compete against others and become the master of popping balloons!

Features - Colorful sceneries with funny emojis designs. - Power ups will appear along the map to help you boost your speed. - Beware of obstacles, they will stun you if you crash on them! - See the global chart and hit the number 1 position. - Unlock different and curious tools for popping balloons.
